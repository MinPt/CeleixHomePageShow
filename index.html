About

Founded in 2015, Celeix Corp is a growing niche engineering and technology company focused on servicing the design and development needs of our customers-as well as delivering innovative solutions that revolutionize your operations and empower you to do what you do best, even better. Celeix maintains an organizational culture that attracts best-in-class talent, building technology platforms and applications that transform consumer lives by addressing everyday problems with scalable ideas and lasting impact. We believe that best-as well as next-practices, adopted with disciplined management, strategic thinking, and focused execution, can yield not just high-quality results, but exceptional return on investment
